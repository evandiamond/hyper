Dr. Marga

Case Study

Overview

Marga F. Massey, MD, CLT, FACS is a board certified Plastic Surgeon committed to care of cancer survivors. With offices in 4 states, she provides care to individuals suffering the ill effects of breast implant failures, microsurgical flap failures, irradiation injury and lymphedema.

Objectives

We helped Dr. Marga take her ideas and create an inspirational portal for woman around the world. Our objective was to educate the world through story and bold visuals.

Stories of her beautiful Mermaids help tell the story of perseverance through troubling times. The stories are heartwarming, but uplifting.

Dr. Marga called on us to help maintain many aspects of her Website, including marketing materials, print, advertisements in Delta Magazine and more. We even helped on location at a few of her “The Doctor Is In” events.

Final Product

Dr. Marga is such an incredible inspiration. She is a skilled surgeon who has helped hundreds of woman across the globe.

At the end of the day, we helped Dr. Marga exceed her goals, ramp up new patients, create a dazzling spectacle of social media, campaigns and more. We love working with Dr. Marga and her team of professionals.

We are honored to help with such a worthy, world-class organization.
